Do you have depression?

1. I feel little interest or pleasure in doing things.

2. I'm feeling down and hopeless.

3. I have trouble falling asleep or I sleep too much.

4. I always feel tired or no energy.

5. I don't have appetite or I tend to overeat.

6. I feel bad about myself, feeling like a failure and have let everyone down.

7. I have trouble concentrating.

8. I speak and move so slowly that other people noticed.

9. I have thoughts that I would be better off dead, or I hurt myself.

10. How difficult have these problems made it for me when I am at work, home, school, or with other people?
